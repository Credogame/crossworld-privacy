Privacy Policy — Crossworld

Effective Date: June 26, 2025

Owner: Credo

1. General Information

Crossworld is a free game monetized through advertising. We do not collect any personal data directly, but we use the Google AdMob SDK, which may automatically collect certain data as described below.

2. What Data AdMob SDK Collects

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and transmit the following data:

  • Device identifiers – such as Android Advertising ID, App Set ID, or other device-specific IDs.
  • IP address – used to determine approximate location.
  • Diagnostic data – app crash reports, launch times, user interactions (clicks/views).
  • Ad data – ad content, impressions, clicks, and performance metrics.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

Collected data is used for:

  • Personalized advertising
  • Advertising performance analysis
  • SDK performance improvement and fraud prevention

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

The collected information may be shared with:

  • Google and its advertising partners for personalized ads
  • Other third-party advertising or analytics networks through the AdMob platform

5. Data Security

All data is transmitted over secure connections (TLS/HTTPS) and handled in accordance with Google’s security policies.

6. Managing Advertising Identifiers

Users can opt out of ad personalization or reset their advertising ID in Android settings (Google Settings → Ads). In Crossworld, this relies on system-level controls.

7. Consent for Personalized Ads (GDPR, EU)

For users in the EU, we use the Google UMP SDK to request explicit consent before showing personalized ads. Without consent, only non-personalized ads will be shown or ad settings will respect system preferences.

8. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Opt out of ad tracking or reset their ad ID
  • Disable personalized ads via Android or Google settings
  • Contact the developer to request data deletion or information
  • Modify or withdraw consent in GDPR regions
  • Review Google’s privacy policy: How Google uses data

9. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this policy (e.g., if new SDKs or data collection methods are added). The effective date at the top will reflect the latest version.
